About Me

I am a third year Psychological Science major at Illinois Institute of Technology. I was recruited by the head coach of the women's soccer team to play and continue my education. I am originally from San Diego county in California. I grew up in a town called Encinitas and within that a community called Leucadia. It's a beautiful coastal town that has a lot of sunshine, beaches, and tourist. The reason I came all the way out to Chicago was to experience diversity and to begin my journey through life in a new fantastic city where I could get the most out of my college years. By that I mean learn to do things on my own, meet new people, and simply discover who I want to be and what I want to do. It was definitely a drastic change moving to the midwest but at the same time its been an adventure. My first winter ive ever experienced was a polar vortex! its was scary at the time but now just a crazy memory of my first actual winter in Chicago. Not many people where im from can say that. Ive been so happy here for the last three years and cant wait to keep my journey going.
